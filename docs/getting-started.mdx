## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Complete KYC

1) Once logged in, begin the KYC process by tapping on 'Send' or 'Withdraw' in the Wallet page

:::warning **Important: KYC Required**
KYC is mandatory to ensure compliance with local laws and regulations. You will **not be able to onramp, send or withdraw funds** until you have completed KYC.
:::
