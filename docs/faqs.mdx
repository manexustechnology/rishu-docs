# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some of the most common questions users have about using **Rishu**.

---

## 💳 How do I deposit money into Rishu?

You can deposit using a variety of supported payment methods, such as:

- Google Pay
- Credit/Debit Cards
- Local payment channels (varies by country)

Simply open the app, tap on **"Deposit"**, and follow the on-screen instructions.

---

## 📈 Which tokens and networks does Rishu support?

Rishu currently supports token purchases on:

- **Katana Mainnet** (primary network)
- **Polygon Mainnet**

You can view the available tokens for each network directly in the **"Buy Tokens"** section of the app.

## 🔐 Is KYC (identity verification) required?

Yes. For security and compliance reasons, all users are required to complete a **KYC verification** before making their first deposit or purchase.

This helps us prevent fraud and comply with regulatory guidelines.

---

## 🗑️ How do I delete my Rishu account?

We're sorry to see you go. If you’d like to permanently delete your account:

1. Open the **Rishu App**.
2. Go to **Settings** in the top right.
3. Scroll down and tap on **Legal & Privacy**.
4. Then Tap on **"Delete Account"**.
