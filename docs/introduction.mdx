# Introduction to Rishu 🚀

Welcome to **Rishu** – your gateway to seamless crypto investing, built with everyday users in mind.

Rishu is a mobile-first application that empowers users to effortlessly buy and manage cryptocurrencies using familiar payment methods like **Google Pay**, **Credit Cards**, and other local **on-ramp** services.

Our goal is to make crypto accessible to everyone — even users without prior blockchain experience. With Rishu, getting started with crypto is as easy as shopping online.

## 🔑 Key Features

- **Simple Onboarding**: No complicated wallets or technical jargon. Sign up and start depositing in minutes.
- **One-Tap Token Purchase**: Use your local currency to buy selected tokens in-app without needing an external exchange.
- **Network Support**: Currently supports **Katana Mainnet** (primary) and **Polygon Mainnet**.
- **Secure and Compliant**: We partner with trusted on-ramp providers to ensure KYC compliance and secure transactions.
- **Mobile First**: Built natively for Android and iOS for the best user experience.

## 💡 Why Rishu?

Most crypto apps assume users already understand exchanges, wallets, and blockchain. **Rishu is different** — it's designed for the average user who simply wants to **invest in tokens quickly and safely**, without friction.

Whether you’re a first-time crypto investor or a casual user looking for convenience, Rishu offers a modern and intuitive experience.

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. **Login/Sign Up** – Login or Create an account with your email.
2. **Verify Identity** – Complete KYC using your ID or passport.
3. **Deposit** – Add funds using Google Pay, credit card, or supported local payment methods.
4. **Buy Tokens** – Choose from a curated list of supported tokens and complete your purchase in seconds.
